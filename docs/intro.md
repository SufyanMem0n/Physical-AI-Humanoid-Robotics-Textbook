# Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

This is an AI-native textbook for the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: 13-Week Industry Course". This professional educational platform is designed for industry practitioners, including engineers, researchers, and developers, providing production-ready content.

## Course Overview

The course is structured into four main modules, each covering critical aspects of modern robotics and AI.

### Part I: Foundations (Weeks 1-3)
This part covers the fundamentals of Physical AI and the Robot Operating System (ROS 2), the standard for robotics software development.
- **Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI**
- **Chapter 2: ROS 2 for Beginners**
- **Chapter 3: Advanced ROS 2**

### Part II: Digital Twins (Weeks 4-6)
Learn how to create and use digital twins for robot simulation and testing, a crucial step before deploying to physical hardware.
- **Chapter 4: Robot Modeling with URDF**
- **Chapter 5: Simulation with Gazebo**
- **Chapter 6: Advanced Gazebo**

### Part III: NVIDIA Isaac Platform (Weeks 7-9)
Dive into the powerful NVIDIA Isaac ecosystem for GPU-accelerated robotics simulation and AI model training.
- **Chapter 7: Introduction to NVIDIA Isaac**
- **Chapter 8: Isaac Sim for Photorealistic Simulation**
- **Chapter 9: Reinforcement Learning with Isaac Gym**

### Part IV: VLA & Humanoid Robotics (Weeks 10-13)
Explore the cutting edge of AI in robotics with Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models and their application to complex humanoid robots.
- **Chapter 10: Vision-Language-Action Models**
- **Chapter 11: Bipedal Locomotion**
- **Chapter 12: Whole-Body Manipulation**
- **Chapter 13: The Future of Humanoid Robots**
